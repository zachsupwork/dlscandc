About Us

Who We Are

DLS has been operating since 1988. The owner, Luciano, has been working in the trades for the last 49 years. Our small crew has a combined experience of 142 years. This extensive experience has led to our saying: "Show me the job in 10 years, and if it still looks nice, I will be happy."

What We Do

We specialize in restoration and repairs, offering a full range of residential improvements as well as commercial work. Our services include interior and exterior projects, foundation and concrete repairs, all aimed at effective water management around the building envelope. DLS is fully insured with an excellent safety record.

Why Choose Us?

We work on homes and structures of all ages, bringing considerable knowledge and understanding of how everything in the building envelope—from the roof to the foundation—ties together. Proper management of the building envelope, including water and moisture management, will maximize your home's overall health and lifespan.
